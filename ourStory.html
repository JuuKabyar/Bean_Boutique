Our Story

Welcome to Bean Boutique! Each cup is crafted with care so every visit feels like home. Our journey began with a simple love for coffee and a dream to create a place where people could come together, relax and enjoy the delicious taste of fresh coffee.

Founded in 1999,Bean Boutique was born out of our passion for high-quality coffee and our desire to build a community hub in Yangon. We believe that great coffee is more than just a beverage, it's an experience.

That's why we source our beans from sustainable farms to ensure that each cup is not only delicious, but ethically supported. Whether it's to taste an original espresso, relax with a caffe latte, or catch up with friends in a cozy corner, Our Bean Boutique is here to make those moments special. But we are more than just a coffee shop. We're proud to be a part of a community that hosts everything from open mic nights to coffee-making workshops.

Our goal is to create a place where innovation, conversation and connection thrive, where everyone is welcome. So come on in, grab your favorite coffee and be a part of our story. I can't wait to share my passion for coffee with you.
